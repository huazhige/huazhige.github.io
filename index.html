My name is Huazhi Ge.


I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate. I study planetary science in Department of Earth and Planetary Science, University of California, Santa Cruz. I work with Prof. Xi Zhang at UCSC and Prof. Cheng Li at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor to study climate and atmospheric dynamics on solar system planets, exoplanets, and brown dwarfs. I also study the protoplanetary disk with Prof. Zhaohuan Zhu to unveil the history of the solar system.
