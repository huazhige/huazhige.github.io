  My name is Huazhi Ge (葛华志).


  I am a 51 Pegasi b Postdoctoral Research Fellow in planetary science. I study planetary science in the Division of Geological and Planetary Sciences, Caltech. 


  I graduated from University of Science and Technology of China with my bachelor degree in 2017 and University of California, Santa Cruz with my doctorate degree in 2023. My Ph.D advisors are Prof. Xi Zhang and Prof. Cheng Li.


  I am now working with Prof. Andrew Ingersoll to study climate and atmospheric dynamics on solar system planets, exoplanets, and brown dwarfs. I am also interested in planetary formation and protoplanetary disks.
